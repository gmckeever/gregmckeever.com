Nightstand

This has been one of my most challenging works. Inspired by Harvey Ellis and other Arts & Crafts era woodworkers, this is a dramatic take on redefining the nightstand.

There are two sliding dovetail joints (connecting the top to the sides), pop-out hinges for the vertical doors, and brass inlay that is an original design. The hardware (handles) are cast brass from England.
